Fluffy pancakes made with ripe bananas — naturally sweet and great for breakfast or a weekend treat.

## Ingredients

- 2 ripe bananas
- 2 eggs
- Pinch of salt
- Butter, for frying

## Method

1. Mash the bananas in a bowl until smooth.
2. Beat in the eggs and salt until combined.
3. Melt a small knob of butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
4. Pour small rounds of batter into the pan. Cook for 2 minutes until the edges look set.
5. Flip and cook for another 1–2 minutes until golden.
6. Serve with maple syrup, sliced banana, or fresh berries.

## Notes

The riper the bananas, the sweeter the pancakes. These are naturally gluten-free. For a slightly fluffier result, add ¼ tsp baking powder to the batter.
